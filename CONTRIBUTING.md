# Contributing

Reproducible results are welcome. Please include:

- GPU model and VRAM;
- operating system, driver, PyTorch, CUDA, ComfyUI, and custom-node versions;
- exact model and quantization filenames;
- resolution, steps, sampler, scheduler, CFG, denoise, seed, and prompt;
- separate cold and warm end-to-end timings;
- unedited output images or hashes;
- a short note about identity, text, geometry, and artifact quality.

Do not commit model weights, private images, access tokens, or outputs you do not have permission to publish. Before opening a pull request, confirm that the relevant model and asset licenses allow the proposed use.
